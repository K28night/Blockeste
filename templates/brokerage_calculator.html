About Brokerage

What is Brokerage?

Brokerage is a fee charged by the platform for facilitating the transaction. In this case, the brokerage is fixed at 1% of the property cost in ETH.

How is Brokerage Calculated?

The brokerage is calculated as 1% of the property cost in ETH. For example, if the property cost is 10 ETH, the brokerage will be 0.10 ETH.

How is the Final Amount Calculated?

The final amount is calculated by subtracting the brokerage from the property cost. For example, if the property cost is 10 ETH and the brokerage is 0.10 ETH, the final amount will be 9.90 ETH.

How Does Currency Conversion Work?

You can select a currency (INR, USD, EUR) to display the final amount. The conversion rates are based on current market values. For example, if 1 ETH = 200,000 INR, then 9.90 ETH will be displayed as 1,980,000 INR.
